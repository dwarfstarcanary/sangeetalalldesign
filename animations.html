CSS Animations

Active Animation

If you click and hold on the star logo at the top left corner on the Homepage , the star does a little dance across the screen.


Hover Animation

If you hover over the navigation buttons at the top of the Homepage , the buttons change color and become smaller.

Infinite Animation

The rectangles and square create a "glitch" effect as they continuously move across the "Initializing Panel" on the Homepage.
