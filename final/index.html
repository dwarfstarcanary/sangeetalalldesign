What is the Butterfly Effect?

"The butterfly effect is the idea that small things can have non-linear impacts on a complex system. The concept is imagined with a butterfly flapping its wings and causing a typhoon."
– Rhiannon Beaubien

Every choice we make impacts where we end up in the future event, no matter how small the decision.

Decision making games are my favorite genre of video games. It's interesting to see how the choices I make impact how the story progresses. It requires me to take a step back and think about how my decisions will affect all of the characters.

Several examples of games in this genre include "Life is Strange", "The Quarry", and "Until Dawn".
