About me

I, (Sangeeta Lall), am an aspiring director/screenwriter and game designer. I love art, video games, films, and all things space related. My favorite genres are science fiction, mystery, and horror. My all time favorite film is Interstellar and my all time favorite series is Stranger Things. When it comes to video games Life is Strange is perfect in my eyes.

Aspirations

Growing up, I have always loved the art of storytelling. I aspire to tell my own stories through films and video games. At Hunter, I am a film major with an emerging media minor. My goals are to build a portfolio and to strengthen my writing skills. I would love to be part of the entertainment industry, more specifically as a game design concept artist or a screenwriter. My goal for senior year is to intern at Riot Games.

Hobbies

If I'm not usually watching a movie or playing a game, I am most likely found hanging out with my niece. I love showing her how to paint and draw. I also enjoy going out to concerts, reading, skateboarding, and making films with my own DSLR.
